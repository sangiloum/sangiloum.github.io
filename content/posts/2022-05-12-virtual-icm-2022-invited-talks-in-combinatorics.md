For convenience, I’ve collected the [list of invited speakers for Section 13 Combinatorics](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124215004/https://icm2022.org/sections/section-13-combinatorics) at the Virtual ICM 2022 and [the schedule of their talks](https://www.mathunion.org/icm/icm-2022/virtual-icm-2022-program-sections). (I’ve omitted speakers whose primary section is not Combinatorics.)
